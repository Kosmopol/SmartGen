## **2. Edge AI and IoT Integration**

Edge AI, the practice of processing data locally on devices, is gaining momentum as the Internet of Things (IoT) continues to expand. By bringing AI capabilities to the edge of the network, devices can make real-time decisions without relying on cloud connectivity. This not only enhances efficiency but also addresses privacy and security concerns associated with centralized data processing. The integration of Edge AI with IoT devices is driving innovation in smart homes, autonomous vehicles, and industrial automation, creating a more interconnected and intelligent ecosystem.
